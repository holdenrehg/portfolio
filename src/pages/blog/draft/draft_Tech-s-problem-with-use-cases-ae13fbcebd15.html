Tech’s problem with use cases

Some iseas are unanimously adopted throughout most of the industry. These often are and should be simple ideas.

Because they are simple answers, we naturally fill in the blanks ourselves to apply to our specific situations. This can create more issues at worst or not solve our problems leaving us where we started at best.

The adoption of use cases in project planning and feature development is one of those ideas.

I feel like we just replaced “make up features we think users would like” to “make up features we think users would like and word them as a use case”.
