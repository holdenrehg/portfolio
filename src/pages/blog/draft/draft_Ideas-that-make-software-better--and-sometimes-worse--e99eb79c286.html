Balance

There are a ton of different variables in play when developing an application. If you are force into a situation making a choice between trade off’s, what do you do? Is readability more valuable in that scenario than performance? Is test-ability more valuable in that scenario than readability? etc.

As developers and architects, where are those lines?

Integrity

Essentially these are concepts that revolve around the idea of “does my program do what it is supposed to?”

  • Data, every program is about ultimately input, transformation, output.
  • Context, the context of where, who, when, how data is entered, stored, accessed, transformed, or outputted matters a lot.


Workflow

A huge amount of the “code issues” that we deal with and worry about day to day have nothing to do with the software functioning as expected or even with the customer’s interactions with the software.

Many of our issues as developers revolve around dealing with maintainability so that we are able to work together as a development team to continue to work on the software.

  • Abstraction, I seem to be in a constant struggle between what should and shouldn’t be abstracted. Where’s the line?
