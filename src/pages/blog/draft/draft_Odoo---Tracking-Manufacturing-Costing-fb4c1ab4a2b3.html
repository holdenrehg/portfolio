Manufacturing Costing Flows / Account Structure In Odoo 10

We are going to take a look at all the cost factors that go into manufacturing a physical good, how those cost factors flow through the Odoo 10 inventory system, and how the accounting system links up / reports on those specific costs.

A Sample Manufacturing Company

Our example is looking at the company Stingray Manufacturing. They manufacturing the following goods:

  • Stingray Model A Automobile — 4 Door Sedan

The Stingray Model A consists of the following BOM:

  • 1 Base, $15,000 each
  • 1 Body, $3,560 each
  • 4 Fenders, $200 each
  • 4 Tires, $350 each


Account Structures

We are going to break out our chart of accounts into 3 inventory account for raw goods, work in process, and finished goods. Then we will track costs of goods manufactured and our cost of goods sold.
